Team

Wim Soons

Licentiehouder

wim@tedxmechelen.be

Wim Soons is a policy economist and political scientist, and works as an advisor in the Flemish Parliament. As a fan of the TEDx concept, he took the initiative to organize the conference in Mechelen. Wim regularly writes opinion based contributions for De Morgen, Knack.be and a number of opinion-related websites

Tom proost

Production & Tech

tom@tedxmechelen.be

Tom Proost has been passionate about music, sound and technology ever since he was a child. After his studies to become an industrial engineer, he trained in London to gain more in-depth knowledge of production and technology. He currently combines both worlds. He works in an R & D team at NXP software. Next to this he mixes as an audio engineer both live and in the studio for, among others, Raymond van het Groenewoud. For a long time fascinated by TED, he did translations in the past and now he goes for the challenge with TEDxMechelen.

Steven Dierckx

Facilities

steven@tedxmechelen.be

Steven Dierckx is a busy, creative generalist who likes to roll up his sleeves and get his hands dirty. He has a background in engineering and construction, which he likes to use both professionally and in his spare time with his own renovation project. For many years he was co-organizer of a local festival, has been growing Christmas trees for ten years and is part of an investors club. So Steven can be described as having an entrepreneurial spirit with a passion for organizing. This in combination with his love for new ideas and innovation makes him an ideal candidate to put his shoulders under TEDx-Mechelen.

Chris Beuckeleers

Speakers

chris@tedxmechelen.be

Chris is the CEO of a sales consultancy company that provides commercial and strategic support for IT companies. With his education in IT and more than 10 years of experience in sales, combined, he gained the experience to support others in this. In addition, he is involved in organizations such as Toastmasters and JCI, in which he has had positions in the board of directors. By constantly looking for innovation, he came into contact with the TEDx concept a few years ago. It is ultimately through the establishment of an event in his hometown Mechelen that led him to participate.

Hans Verbist

IT & Volunteers

Hans@tedxmechelen.be

Hans Verbist started as a graduate in Informatica in education, responsible for the management and expansion of the IT infrastructure for a large number of Mechelen schools. After more than 12 years of experience in this sector, his interest within the IT sector turned mainly to IT security. Here he has also adjusted his professional goals and exchanged his previous job for that of cyber security expert. In addition, Hans is organizing the National Children’s Cancer Day for which he has been committed for years without any interest. His knowledge of event management is an important link where he can provide added value for TEDxMechelen.
